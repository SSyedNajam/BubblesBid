About Bubblesbid

Bubblesbid is a social commerce startup developed to support local and international products for marketing online.

Bubblesbid allows users to search outstanding offers from local businesses around the area where the customer lives.

One of the goals of the company is to sustain and promote local businesses, and help provide easy access to technological platforms in promoting their products and services.

Do you know that by 2021 the global social commerce market will increase by about 34%? Moreover, some researchers forecast that e-sales will exceed $735 billion by 2023.

Every day in fact many buyers are looking and beliveve in referrals and recommendations.

Give a chance to your because to be more accessible and visible helping your new customers to descoverspecial deals and promotions.

StartUp In Evolution

Started in 2020, Bubblesbid wants to operate as a social commercial site, providing a new era of digital shopping online.

Constantly under optimization and development, our commercial cloud platform is ready as your perfect partner online.

We are at the beginning of our journey and we are open to discuss business partnerships and beyond, putting our technological and domain skills at your service.
